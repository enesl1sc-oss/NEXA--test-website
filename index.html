Not at all. Our no-code templates and guided resources are built for non-technical founders and operators. If you can use a spreadsheet, you can automate with NEXA.

We cover 100+ tools including n8n, Make.com, Zapier, OpenAI, Claude, Airtable, Notion, Slack, Stripe, HubSpot, and many more. New integrations are added by the community weekly.

Very active. With 4,200+ members across 30+ countries, our Discord runs 24/7. We host weekly live workshops, monthly expert AMAs, and quarterly showcase events for members to share their work.
